Description




Lasagna alla Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • pre-made sfoglia for lasagne 250 g

  • besciamella 400 g

  • Grated parmesan cheese 400

  • Sweet bacon 250 g

  • Carrots 1

  • Onions 1

  • Celery 1

  • Minced beef 500 g

  • Red wine 200 ml

  • Tomato sauce 400 ml

  • Milk 200 ml

  • Extra virgin olive oil enough

  • Salt enough

  • Pepper enough

Steps

  1. Put the sweet bacon in a pan and begin to fry it alone.
    Let it go in the pan at high flame untill the fat is melted

  2. Let's add the olive oil and all the vegetables, that we previously minced, in the pan and fry them for some minutes, stiring them lively

  3. Put the minced beef, separating it a little and spreading it in the pan uniformely. After 5 minutes add the red wine to the pan and continue for 5 other minutes

  4. Add the tomato sauce, the salt, and give an energic stir

  5. Let it cook at minimum heat for at leat an hour and half, covering tha pan with a lid

  6. When the rag첫 is ready, we can start assembly the lasagna. Put in the base of a pirofila some spoon of rag첫

  7. Add a sfoglia layer and cover it with other rag첫

  8. Let some besciamella spoon slit onto the rag첫 covering

  9. Sprinkle some parmesan cheese upon everything

  10. Make another sfoglia layer and repeat these steps untill there are ingredients. Put some extra cheese at the ending layer so it will become more crispy

  11. Put it in the oven at 180 degrees for 40 minutes

  12. When ready let it rest a little so it will settle, and then serve it
